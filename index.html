— Approach

Principal capital, deployed where alignment permits.

i.
Principal Capital

We invest our own balance sheet — long-horizon, no fund clock, no outside mandate to satisfy. That posture lets us hold through cycles and write the size of check the opportunity calls for.

ii.
Concentration

The bulk of our work is in energy and real estate, where our experience runs deepest and our operator relationships are strongest — minerals, working interests, and value-add real estate among them.

iii.
Selective Opportunities

Outside our core areas, we evaluate opportunities case by case — private credit, operating businesses, and structured positions among them. The bar is the same as everywhere else.

— On Approach
We are not in the business of turning capital over. We are in the business of placing it well, and letting time do most of the work.
